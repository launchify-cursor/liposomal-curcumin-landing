Liposomal Curcumin+®

Bioavailability

Most curcumin supplements—powders, capsules, and tablets—are poorly absorbed during digestion, with up to 95% excreted before reaching your bloodstream. Our proprietary Liposomal Delivery System is engineered to protect curcumin from stomach acid, digestive enzymes, and bile.

Liposomal Technology

Our advanced phospholipid encapsulation system creates nanoscale lipid vesicles that protect curcumin through the harsh digestive environment: the phospholipid outer layer shields the curcuminoid core through the stomach and upper GI tract, while the liquid soft gel format ensures precise dosing and maximum stability.

  • Phospholipid outer layer made from sunflower lecithin protects curcuminoids as they travel through the GI tract.
  • No synthetic coatings—plant-based liposomal formula unlike piperine or pepper extract delivery systems.
  • Engineered for precision delivery directly to cells, where curcumin is needed to work effectively.

Absorption Comparison Testing

To evaluate the absorption of our liposomal curcumin, we use validated pharmacokinetic studies—the gold standard for measuring bioavailability. These studies measure plasma curcumin concentrations over time, comparing liposomal delivery to standard curcumin formulations.
